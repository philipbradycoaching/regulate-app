Regulate helps you expand your window of tolerance — your capacity to experience difficult emotions without being overwhelmed or shutting down. Each session builds this capacity.
When we encounter emotions we've learned to avoid, our nervous system reacts — fight, flight, or freeze. By practicing noticing these sensations and staying present with them, you gradually widen the range of experiences you can handle while remaining grounded. This is how lasting emotional resilience is built.
